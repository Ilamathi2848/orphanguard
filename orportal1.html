John's Journey

John arrived at Sunshine Orphanage when he was just 5 years old. Through love, education, and care, he has now excelled in school and dreams of becoming a doctor to help others in need.

Mary's Story

After facing a difficult childhood, Mary found solace at Sunshine Orphanage. She is now a talented artist, and her paintings are featured in local galleries.

David's Dream

David always had a passion for sports. With the support from Sunshine Orphanage, he is now competing at national-level athletics and has won numerous medals.
